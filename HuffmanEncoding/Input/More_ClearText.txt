Spring is here, but Washington skiers and snowboarders still have a couple more chances to enjoy the mountains this year. 
When you travel to Yosemite Mariposa County, you’re free to roam thousands of square miles in and out of Yosemite National Park filled with photogenic and historic small towns, fishing, rafting, mountain biking and more.
Huge mountains, rugged foothills, deep canyons, vast caverns, and the world’s largest trees exemplify the diversity of landscapes, life, and beauty here. Explore these pages to plan your visit or to learn about the plants and animals here and the threats they face. Ancient giant sequoias may seem invincible, but they, too are vulnerable.
